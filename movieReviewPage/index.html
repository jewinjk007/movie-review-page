Synopsis

In a near-future Earth ravaged by environmental collapse and dwindling resources, former NASA pilot Joseph Cooper is recruited for a secret mission to find a new home for humanity. Traveling through a wormhole near Saturn, Cooper and a team of scientists explore distant planets in another galaxy, each offering hope but also peril. As they contend with harsh worlds, time dilation, and personal sacrifice, Cooper must make impossible choices to save both his crew and his family back on Earth. Blending science, emotion, and the vast unknown, Interstellar is a gripping journey about survival, love, and humanity's place in the cosmos.

As the crew faces alien worlds, time distortions, and the limits of human endurance, Interstellar delivers a breathtaking blend of science, emotion, and cosmic adventure.

Christopher Nolan combines hard science with heartfelt storytelling, delivering an epic that's as much about love and human connection as it is about space travel.
